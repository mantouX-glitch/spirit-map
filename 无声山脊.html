I have reached the spine of the silence.

云海沉在脚下，群峰失语，唯有它在风里醒着。

点击舱体 · 唤醒　—　点击空中 · 聚拢碎片

Here was me buried. Digital my ashes.

Whispering deeply, would you miss me?

My distant messiah. I don’t know you.

Frostbite my lips. I will not be with you.

That I lived in half. Perish in eternity.

In the End. Love against fear. I love you. Or from the very beginning.
